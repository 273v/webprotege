WebProtégé Privacy Policy

We abide by the Standford Core Privacy Policy. In addition to this we aide by the information below.

Information Sharing

We reserve the right to share and distribute OBFUSCATED versions of your ontology and change history. By obfuscated, we mean that we remove all domain information by replacing entity (class, property and individual) names with meaningless identifiers (C1, C2, C3 etc.) and we remove any trace of user identifying information from the change history. Once obfuscated, only the original structure of the ontology and change history remains. We may redistribute this obfuscated data for research purposes.

We will NOT share or redistribute any original unobfuscated ontologies or content that you upload to, or edit at, webprotege.stanford.edu. This includes project change history, discussion threads and other project data.

Account and Data Removal

The WebProtégé user interface does not offer a mechanism for account or project content removal. If you would like us to delete your account and/or project content please send an email to webprotege-management@lists.stanford.edu.
